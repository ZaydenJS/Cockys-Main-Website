FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our painting and decorating services

How long does a typical painting project take?

Project duration depends on size and complexity. A single room typically takes 1-2 days, while a whole house can take 1-2 weeks. We'll provide a detailed timeline with your quote.

Do you provide free quotes?

Yes! We offer free, no-obligation quotes for all projects. We'll assess your space and provide detailed pricing and timeline information.

What type of paint do you use?

We use only premium quality paints from leading brands. We'll recommend the best paint type for your specific project and budget requirements.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes, we are fully licensed contractors with comprehensive liability and workers' compensation insurance for your complete peace of mind.

Do you offer warranties?

We stand behind our work with comprehensive warranties ranging from 1-5 years depending on the service package and materials used.

Can you work around my schedule?

Absolutely! We offer flexible scheduling including evenings and weekends to minimize disruption to your daily routine.
